Pumpkin Bread.

Give Them Pumpkin To Talk About!

Ingredientes

      Dry Ingredients.
  • 2 cups of flour (I used oat flour)
  • 1 Teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 Tablespoon of pumpkin Spice
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
      Wet Ingredients.
  • 15 oz of Pumpkin Puree
  • 75 ml of Dairy-free Milk (I used soy)
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoon of Flax egg
  • 1 tablespoon of any nut butter
    For the topins, I used chopped nuts

Preparation

  1. Starting with your dry ingredients, put the oat flour and the pumpkin spices on a bowl as well as the baking soda. Add some salt and mix it up well.
  2. For your wet ingredients; on a different bowl add the flax eggs, the brown sugar, the pumpkin puree and the tablespoon of you favorite nut butter; (I used almond butter).
  3. Mix it up very well with the help of a silicone spatula.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the bowl that contains the dry ingredients and keep mixing until you get a thick compound.
  5. Coat the entire inside of the tray that you will use with vegan butter or margarine, or you can just use parchment paper.
  6. You should always preheat the oven to 375 degree Farenheit.
  7. With the spatula help pour the mix into the tray, and place in the oven. (All components of the tray must be oven-proof)
  8. Finally, wait about 20 to 30 minutes until you notice they will come nice and fragrant.
